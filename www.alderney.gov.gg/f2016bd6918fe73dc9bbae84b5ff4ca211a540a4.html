Matthew Birmingham

Elected to Office in November 2014 for a 4 year term. First elected to office in November 2010.

Responsibilities as of January 2016:

  • Member, Policy and Finance Committee
  • Chairman, Building and Development Control Committee
  • Member, Tourism and Marketing Committee (sub committee of Policy and Finance Committee)
  • Alternate Representative Guernsey States of Deliberation

Individual responsibilities from the Policy and Finance Committee Committee:

  • States Appointed Director, Alderney Electricity Limited
  • Member, Alderney eGambling Limited
