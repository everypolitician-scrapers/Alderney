Graham McKinley

Elected in November 2014 for a 4 year term (ending 31 Dec 2018).

Responsibilities as of January 2018:

  • Member, Policy and Finance Committee
  • Member, General Services Committee
  • Member, Commonwealth Parliamentary Association
  • Representative, Guernsey States of Deliberation

Individual Responsibilities within the Policy and Finance Committee:-

  • Liaison, Health & Social Care Committee
  • Liaison, Employment & Social Security Committee

Individual Responsibilities within the General Services Committee:-

  • Harbour & Fisheries / Council of The Alderney Maritime Trust
  • Marina Development
